Nyan Dash

Created By: Juno Cheung

In Nyan Dash, you take control of the beloved Nyan Cat as it zooms across a vibrant, pixelated landscape. Avoid quirky obstacles like bushes, fire hydrants, and cat posts while leaping through the air to keep the fun going. With simple one-button controls, anyone can jump right into this addictive endless runner and aim for the highest score. Perfect for fans of fast-paced gameplay and the iconic Nyan Cat, this game offers an exciting challenge with retro visuals and a fun, engaging atmosphere.

Instructions:

Objective:

  • The goal of Nyan Dash is to keep Nyan Cat running as long as possible by avoiding obstacles that appear randomly across the path.
  • Every successful jump over an obstacle increases your score. See how high you can score before the obstacles catch up to you!

Controls:

  • Press the SpaceBar to make Nyan Cat jump over the obstacles.
  • Start the Game: Press the START button after a game over to restart and begin a new run.

Gameplay:

  • Endless Runner: Nyan Cat continuously runs forward along the ground. Your job is to jump and avoid obstacles.
  • Jump to Avoid Obstacles: Randomly generated obstacles like bushes, fire hydrants, and cat posts will appear on the path. Time your jumps carefully to avoid hitting them.
  • Scoring: Every time you successfully leap over an obstacle, your score increases by 1. There’s no upper limit, so keep running to achieve new high scores.
  • Difficulty: The game’s speed gradually increases, requiring faster reactions and more precise jumps to keep Nyan Cat safe.

End Game:

  • If Nyan Cat collides with an obstacle, the game ends. You’ll see a Game Over screen displaying your current score and your all-time highest score.
